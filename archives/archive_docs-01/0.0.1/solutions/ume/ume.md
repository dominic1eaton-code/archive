# business operating system
- business operations,process,system hub/centralized dashboard
    + automate business sytsem/processes to allow optimal use of the business cpu (the business operator(s,team,stakeholders))
    + provide common API for business operations,processes,system(s)
    + a single place where all business tools can interoperate/integrate/come together to provide a unified interface of execution for the business operator(s); the virtual business cpu interface for the physical business cpu(a.k.a the business operator(s))
        + the single unified/modular/extensible/interoperable portal to access and operate a business that has many tools, users, stakeholders, processes/systems and resources to manage (at scale)
        + centralize, unify,consolidate,simplify the interface of business operations,execution
    + written in rust
