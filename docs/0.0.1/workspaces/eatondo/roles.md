* obatala studios
    - VC studio factory
        * Entrepreneur: Studios initiate and refine business ideas, develop market narratives, and make foundational decisions about business models and strategy, essentially creating the company from the ground up.

        * Operator: They take on the day-to-day operations, which can include product development, engineering, and designing the minimum viable product (MVP). They also provide support in areas like marketing, branding, legal, and finance, often using a defined process to help the company reach key milestones faster.

        * Investor: Studios provide early-stage capital and maintain significant ownership in the companies they build. They act as the initial investor but also help the company secure further funding rounds from traditional venture capital firms
