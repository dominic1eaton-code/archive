- Feedback incorporation
- Documentation

- Must be documented

ARTICLE XII ‚Äî AMENDMENTS
These Bylaws may be amended only if:

- Amendments do not violate the Non-Dominance Principle
- Amendments preserve role replaceability
- Amendments are documented and reviewable

ARTICLE XIII ‚Äî DISSOLUTION
Upon dissolution:

- Preference given to aligned educational or research entities
- No private enrichment beyond lawful compensation
FINAL COMPRESSION
These Bylaws exist to prevent authority from replacing understanding,
and speed from replacing coherence.

They are guardrails, not engines.
