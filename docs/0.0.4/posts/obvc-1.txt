OBATALA VENTURE CAPITAL STUDIOS (OVCS)
CHARTER
Version: Phase-0 Draft
Status: Provisional, Revisable
Authority Level: Orienting, not governing

NAME & NATURE

Obatala Venture Capital Studios (OVCS) is a venture-formation and capital-orchestration studio operating as a systems-first ecosystem rather than a monolithic firm.

OVCS is:

A studio, not a holding company

A coordination substrate, not a command center

A portfolio of experiments, not a single strategy

OVCS exists to enable, explore, and stabilize ventures without collapsing their autonomy.

PURPOSE

The purpose of OVCS is to:

• Incubate and explore new organizations
• Provide optional capital pathways
• Preserve long-term optionality
• Reduce systemic risk across ventures
• Protect meaning, care, and learning from extraction
OVCS prioritizes system health over individual optimization.

CORE OPERATING POSTURE

OVCS operates under the following postures:

Systems precede assets

Learning precedes scaling

Reversibility precedes commitment

Coordination precedes control

Care is never instrumentalized

OVCS explicitly rejects:

Growth at all costs

Narrative-driven decision making

Hidden power consolidation

Moral leverage through capital

ROLE OF OVCS IN THE ECOSYSTEM

OVCS may act as:

• Venture studio (formation & incubation)
• Capital studio (hypothetical and real)
• Interface between capital, labor, and care
• Risk buffer for early exploration
OVCS does not act as:

A universal parent authority

An operational manager of all entities

A moral arbiter

A single source of truth

RELATIONSHIP TO SUBSIDIARY & AFFILIATED ENTITIES

Each organization within the OVCS ecosystem:

Retains its own mission, leadership, and decision authority

Has the right to refuse integration, capital, or coordination

May enter and exit relationships with OVCS without penalty

No entity is required to:

Adopt OVCS language

Align narratives

Share tooling

Accept capital

CAPITAL PRINCIPLES

Capital under OVCS is governed by these principles:

• Capital is optional
• Capital does not imply control
• Capital does not buy silence
• Capital does not override care
In Phase 0:

Most capital activity remains hypothetical

No growth mandates are imposed

No timelines are enforced

GOVERNANCE & AUTHORITY

OVCS governance is lightweight and provisional.

Authority is:

Explicit

Scoped

Time-bounded

Revocable

There is no permanent central authority in Phase 0.

Governance mechanisms favor:

Documentation over enforcement

Dissent over consensus

Exit over compliance

RISK, FAILURE & LEARNING

OVCS treats failure as signal, not defect.

Commitments:

Failures are logged, not punished

Risks are surfaced early

Uncertainty is named explicitly

Bad news has priority routing

No individual or entity is penalized for:

Slowing down

Reversing course

Exiting an experiment

CARE, JUSTICE & IRREVERSIBLE DOMAINS

OVCS recognizes certain domains as irreversible or high-harm:

• Community trust
• Reparative justice
• Human dignity
• Public memory
These domains:

Are protected from capital pressure

Cannot be used to stabilize economic risk

Require explicit consent and separation

ANTI-PATTERN COMMITMENTS

OVCS commits to actively detecting and mitigating:

• Power collapse
• Boundary erosion
• Narrative capture
• Capital coercion
• Care instrumentalization
• Premature integration
Detection triggers review, not punishment.

PHASE DISCIPLINE

OVCS operates under explicit phase awareness.

In Phase 0:

Exploration is prioritized

Structure remains provisional

Integration is discouraged

Documentation is mandatory

Transition to later phases requires:

Explicit review

Logged rationale

Reversibility analysis

AMENDMENT & EVOLUTION

This charter:

Is not final

May be revised at any time

Must log meaning changes (SEL)

Must preserve original intent where possible

No amendment may:

Retroactively coerce consent

Remove exit rights

Collapse care into capital

CLOSING ORIENTATION

OVCS exists to make good systems possible, not inevitable.

Its success is measured not only by what it builds,
but by what it chooses not to force.
